Colours

Sapphire (#2635BA)

The site's primary colour. Used as the background on main pages and the accent colour on project pages.

Lemon Juice (#FFFF54)

The main accent colour. A bright yellows that stands out nicely.

Aquamarine (#48F0D2)

A secondary accent colour, mainly used to denote interactivity like hyperlinks and button hover states.

Blanche (#FFFFFF)

Basic white. used as the background for some elements and as paragraph text colour on blue pages.

Slate (#2D2D2F)

A cool, dark grey used as the paragraph text colour on white pages.

Green Grey (#588486)

An understated greenish grey used for subheadings with less hierarchy.
